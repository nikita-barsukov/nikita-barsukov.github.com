Average net household income in Danish municipalities in 2000 - 2012

All numbers are in current Danish kroner. Red line in tooltip is nation average.

Play button starts animation.

You can drag and zoom map.

A while ago I posted a map of average yearly income in Danish municipalities. I put a lot of effort to create that map, but after some time I realized that not just my code was a big mess, but visualisation itself can be improved a great deal. So I sat down, took a new dataset from Danish statistics site, and rewrote the whole visualization.

This viz reflects following key points:

  • Danish wealthy leave almost exclusively in the area to the north of Copenhagen. Wealth in other parts of Denmark is distributed remarkably evenly;
  • No areas in Denmark with average income significantly less than nation average;
  • There was a decline in national income in 2008, especially in the rich areas;
